DELUXE ROOMS

Experience the ultimate in comfort and convenience with our deluxe room accommodations. Your well-being is our priority, and we've designed these rooms to provide you with a delightful and relaxing stay.

Each deluxe room is thoughtfully furnished with a high-quality TV for your entertainment, ensuring you stay connected with the outer world. The water heater provides instant hot water, guaranteeing you a refreshing shower or a relaxing bath at any time.

Our good soft beds are your haven of rest, offering a peaceful night's sleep. You'll wake up refreshed and ready to explore the day.

The clean and stylish bathroom, equipped with high-end fixtures, offers you a private oasis. It's the perfect space to relax and rejuvenate.

COTTAGE

Escape to our luxurious Cottage, a haven of serenity and sophistication. Each deluxe room within the cottage is designed for your utmost comfort and relaxation.

Your entertainment needs are met with a state-of-the-art TV, providing a range of channels for your enjoyment. The water heater ensures you have instant access to warm water, whether for a revitalizing shower or a soothing bath.

Our plush, good soft beds promise a night of deep and restful sleep, and the well-appointed, high-end bathroom offers a clean and stylish space.

Indulge in the tranquil luxury of our Cottage, where these amenities transform your stay into a memorable and rejuvenating experience.
