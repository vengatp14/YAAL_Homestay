Our Policies

Adherence to our simple policies guarantees you a hassle-free and pleasant stay with us.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in time is 11 A.M and Check-out time is 10 A.M. Early check-in / late check-out allowed with extra charges based on next day room availability.

Payment Terms

Advance payment is required for booking confirmation. Remaining balance must be paid during check-in.

Damage Deposit

A refundable damage deposit of Rs.500 will be collected during check-in. It will be returned during checkout if no damages are found.

Room Care

Guests are requested to keep rooms clean and avoid any damages to prevent misunderstandings with the management.

Cancellation Policy

Booking advance will be refunded if cancellation is informed at least 3 days before the check-in date.

Room Rent Variations

Room rent may vary during holidays or peak vacation dates. We always ensure budget-friendly pricing.

Prohibited Activities

Illegal activities are strictly prohibited. If found guilty, management reserves the right to evict guests immediately.
