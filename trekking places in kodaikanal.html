trekking places in kodaikanal

Perumal Peak Trek in Kodaikanal

Perumal Peak is a must-visit trekking spot in Kodaikanal, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the Western Ghats. Ideal for adventure seekers, this moderate trek takes you through lush greenery, rugged terrains, and serene landscapes. Experience the thrill of reaching the summit and witnessing nature's grandeur.

Bear Shola Falls Trek in Kodaikanal

The Bear Shola Falls Trek is perfect for nature lovers seeking a serene escape. This tranquil trek leads to a mesmerizing waterfall surrounded by dense forests. Enjoy the soothing sound of water cascading amidst lush greenery, making it a picturesque and rejuvenating experience.

Dolphin's Nose and Echo Rock Trek

Dolphin's Nose and Echo Rock Trek offers stunning cliff views and thrilling trekking paths. This adventure-packed trek rewards you with panoramic views of lush valleys and misty mountains. The experience is perfect for photographers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Elephant Valley Falls Trek

Explore the serene beauty of Elephant Valley Falls through this captivating trek. The trail offers lush greenery, soothing waterfalls, and a peaceful environment, perfect for a refreshing retreat. The trek is a hidden gem for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

Pillar Rocks Trek in Kodaikanal

The Pillar Rocks Trek is a popular trail offering mesmerizing views of towering rock formations shrouded in mist. This moderate trek takes you through pine forests and scenic landscapes, making it a photographer’s paradise and a nature lover’s dream.

Pine Forest Trek in Kodaikanal

Embark on the Pine Forest Trek and walk amidst towering pine trees that create a serene and enchanting atmosphere. The easy-to-moderate trek is ideal for families and beginners, offering picturesque views and a tranquil escape from the city's hustle.

Silent Valley View Trek in Kodaikanal

The Silent Valley View Trek offers spectacular views of lush valleys and mist-covered hills. Perfect for early morning hikes, this peaceful trail provides a sense of calm and connection with nature, making it a must-visit spot for trekkers.

Vattakanal Trek in Kodaikanal

The Vattakanal Trek is a favorite among adventure seekers, offering trails through dense forests and breathtaking viewpoints. Known for its pristine beauty, this trek is perfect for those seeking solitude and spectacular views of the hills.

Vellagavi Village Trek in Kodaikanal

Discover the charm of the Vellagavi Village Trek, an offbeat trail leading to a picturesque hamlet surrounded by untouched nature. This moderate trek offers a glimpse into traditional village life while immersing you in the beauty of the Western Ghats.
