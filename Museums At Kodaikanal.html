Museums At Kodaikanal

Natural History Museum

The Shembaganur Museum in Kodaikanal offers a fascinating collection of over 300 species of orchids, fossils, and rare wildlife specimens. It highlights the region's rich biodiversity and showcases artifacts from the Palaiyar tribe, providing an educational experience for nature and history enthusiasts.

Sacred Heart College Museum

The Kodaikanal Museum displays artifacts from the region’s history, culture, and local tribal life. It offers insights into the area's heritage, showcasing ancient tools, pottery, and cultural objects, making it an interesting stop for anyone exploring the town's rich past and traditions.

Kodaikanal Solar Observatory Museum

The Kodaikanal Solar Observatory Museum is one of India’s oldest solar research centers. It features telescopes and instruments for studying the Sun and solar phenomena. Visitors can explore exhibits on sunspots and solar activity, with breathtaking views of the surrounding Palani Hills and access to a public telescope.
