Frequently Asked Questions

Planning your stay with us is hassle free. Book rooms, explore special offers, and understand our policies on reservations, cancellation, amenities available, and get to know the convenient travel arrangements all at your fingertips.

  • How do I make a reservation?

    To book a room, visit our website, select your dates, choose your room type, and follow the simple reservation process.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    Cancellation with refund of the advance payment is applicable only if intimation is given earlier (Before 3 days of the check-in date)

  • Are there any special offers or discounts available?

    Yes, Offers are available if Stays extend more than one day or on weekdays

  • What amenities are included in the room rate?

    Wifi, Spacious and convenient parking, Daily Housekeeping, Television, UPS, Hot water Geyser

  • Do you provide Travel Assistance?

    Yes, we provide Travel Assistance to unravel the nature specific areas and rush spots as well. Secured pick up and drop options readily available.

  • Is Campfire available?

    Yes, Campfire is made ready on Guests' requests. (Charges applicable)
